About Euphoria Fest

Computer science department of Rajagiri College of Social Sciences in collaboration with Kochi Metro and Club FM 94.3 hosted a 'Musical Eve' as a part of the National Fest "EUPHORIA 2K22" Sponsored by SANTA MONICA. The function was inaugurated by Dr. Binoy Joseph ,Principal in the presence of Dr.Bindiya M Vargheese Dean of computer science department , other Faculty and students.
